About the Practice:

  • Adult and and pediatric patients with type 1 diabetes are treated at this practice
  • Patients with New Onset type 1 diabetes who are not in Ketoacidosis are welcome to be seen for teaching and treatment immediately after diagnosis.
  • Adult and pediatric patients with hypothyroid will also be treated.

Services Include:

  • Education of new onset patients and/or parents will be comprehensive.
  • Care and treatment of diabetes includes starting and maintaining treatment with insulin pumps and continuous glucose sensors.
  • New treatments will be considered and best options for each patient are implemented based on input from the patient, family and provider.
